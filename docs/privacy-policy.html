WalkieTalkie Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-03

This Privacy Policy explains how the WalkieTalkie mobile application ("App") collects, uses, and protects data when you use the service.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account information you provide (such as email address and display name).
  • Device and push notification identifiers used for app functionality.
  • Channel and session metadata required to provide voice communication.
  • Microphone audio only while using push-to-talk features.

2. How We Use Data

  • To authenticate users and maintain account sessions.
  • To deliver channel, invite, and push-to-talk functionality.
  • To send essential notifications and service-related events.
  • To improve reliability, security, and abuse prevention.

3. Microphone Permission

The App requests microphone permission (RECORD_AUDIO) to enable push-to-talk voice communication. Audio is used only for this feature while you actively use it.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by infrastructure providers strictly to operate the service (for example hosting, push, and real-time communication services).

5. Data Retention and Security

We retain data only as needed for service operation, legal obligations, and security purposes. Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to protect stored data.

6. Your Choices

  • You can stop using the App at any time.
  • You can revoke permissions from your device settings.
  • You can request account-related support through the contact email below.
